How to play

Guess the mystery player in under eight tries. click the show silhouette button to help narrow down your guess. After each guess, you will be given clues.

  • Green in any category is a match
  • A yellow in the team category means the player used to play for that team.
  • An arrow in the age or jersey number category indicates a higher or lower number
