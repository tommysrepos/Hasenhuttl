How to play

Guess the mystery player in under eight tries. After each guess, you will be given clues.

  • The mystery player and each guess you make must be an active Premier League player with at least 5 appearances this season.
  • click the show silhouette button to help narrow down your guess.
  • Green in any category is a match.
  • Yellow in the team category means the player used to play for that team.
  • ↑ or ↓ in the age or jersey # category indicates a higher or lower number.
